📖 How to Use VoiceQuest
Tips for best results, usage guide, and ethical use policy
🗣️ TTS Clone Mode
🎯 Best Practices

Upload a clear, noise-free audio clip of the voice you want to clone. The AI needs at least 3 seconds of speech — 6–15 seconds gives the best results.

✅ Use WAV, MP3, or OGG files. Mono recordings work best.
✅ Leave the text box empty to automatically clone what the person said in the audio.
✅ Type your own text to make the voice say anything you want.
⚡ Speed 1.0× is natural. Try 0.9× for a more deliberate, clear output.
🌍 Match the Language setting to the language of the text — mismatches cause poor output.
🎭 Smart Mimic Mode
🎯 Best Practices

This mode re-speaks content using someone else's voice identity via zero-shot AI — no model training needed.

① Upload the target voice (who you want to sound like). Longer clips = better voice fingerprint.
② Either type the text to say (fastest), or upload your own recording of what you want said — it will be transcribed first.
✅ If uploading your performance, speak clearly and avoid background noise for accurate transcription.
⚠️ The output accent/language comes from the target voice, not your performance.
🎛️ RVC Mimic Mode
🎯 Best Practices

RVC uses a pre-trained .pth model to apply a voice identity to your audio. This requires a trained model file in assets/weights/.

🎛 Pitch Shift: Set to 0 for same gender. Use +12 (male→female) or -12 (female→male).
🎛 Voice Similarity 0.75 is the recommended default — higher values can sound unnatural.
🎛 Pitch Smoothing 3 is balanced — higher values reduce pitch wobble.
⚠️ Without a .pth model file, this mode returns a 400 error. See the README for how to acquire models.
🔊 Audio Quality Tips
📻 For Best Results
🎙️ Record in a quiet room. Background noise reduces voice fingerprint accuracy significantly.
📏 Keep reference clips between 6–30 seconds. Very long clips are cropped internally.
🔇 The AI automatically applies noise reduction and normalization — you don't need to pre-process audio.
⏱️ Processing takes ~10–30 seconds on GPU, ~60–120 seconds on CPU. Be patient after clicking the button.

  • 🚫Using synthesized voices to deceive, defraud, manipulate, or spread misinformation.
  • 🚫Generating audio for harassment, hate speech, or non-consensual intimate content.
  • 🚫Bypassing any platform's identity verification or security systems using cloned voices.
  • 🚫Commercial use of cloned voices without legal licensing from the voice owner.
✅ Permitted Uses
  • ✅Cloning your own voice for creative, research, or accessibility projects.
  • ✅Educational demos and AI research with proper attribution.
  • ✅Voice preservation for people with medical conditions affecting speech (with consent).
  • ✅Creative projects like audiobooks, games, or films where all parties have given consent.
  • ✅Processing voices where explicit written consent has been obtained from the voice owner.
